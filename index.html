Ping Pong Ball Launcher

Students will design, build and program their own robotic Ping Pong Launcher! Computer design (CAD), electronic circuits and microcontroller programming will be taught, all used to control all the launcher's motors using a single joystick.

What Your Kids Learn ?

Hands-On Robot Design and 3D Printing

Hands-On Robot Design and 3D Printing

During the camp, kids explore the fascinating process of designing and 3D printing a ping pong ball launcher. They create essential components such as the feeder tube, gears, and base. Finally, they assemble these parts to construct their very own functional launcher

Coding and Electronics

Coding and Electronics

The launcher is equipped with a joystick for controlling elevation and launch, along with motors that move the launcher up and down and launch the ball. Kids gain insights into how sensors, such as the joystick, are utilized to control the motors and shoot the balls

Robot Motors and Sensors

Robot Motors and Sensors

To enable joystick control of the launcher, students learn how to program a microcontroller in the C language. The microcontroller receives joystick commands, allowing them to precisely adjust the launcher’s height and launch the balls

Problem-Solving and Critical Thinking

Problem-Solving and Critical Thinking

Throughout the camp, students encounter diverse mechanical, electronic, and coding challenges. These experiences encourage them to think critically and find solutions, ultimately fostering their confidence in problem-solving
